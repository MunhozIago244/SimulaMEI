# SimulaMEI Design Context

## Register

Brand-led marketing surface with product-grade simulator and dashboard sections.

## Visual Direction

Dark fiscal workspace with high-contrast lime accents, restrained orange/yellow risk signals, and dense but readable operational panels.

## Existing Tokens

- Backgrounds: `var(--bg0)`, `var(--bg1)`, `var(--bg2)`, `var(--bg3)`
- Text: `var(--text1)`, `var(--text2)`, `var(--text3)`
- Accents: `var(--lime)`, `var(--orange)`, `var(--yellow)`, `var(--blue)`
- Radius: `var(--radius)`, `var(--radius-lg)`

## Components

- Simulator-first home page with real form controls.
- Result panels with numeric evidence and status badges.
- Static content pages using `StaticPageLayout`.
- Accountant landing with pricing table and lead form.

## Rules

- Use concrete product previews instead of generic illustrations.
- Keep fiscal numbers scannable with mono values.
- Avoid nested card stacks; use sections, tables, screenshots, and callouts.
- Keep CTAs action-specific: simulate, compare, generate, share, enter list.
